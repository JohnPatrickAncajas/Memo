MEMO

Memo is a user-friendly note-taking platform that allows you to write down, save, and access your notes from anywhere, ensuring you never lose important information again!

How to use?

  • Go to Notes.
  • Write the Title, Date and Task.
  • Add will add the note.
  • Clear will remove the text on the boxes.
  • Edit will bring up aditional options for the notes.
  • Up or Down will move the note.
  • Remove will delete the note.
